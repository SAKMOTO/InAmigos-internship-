Who We Are

A Youth-Driven Movement for Social Good

Founded on September 23, 2020, InAmigos Foundation operates across India to address core social and environmental issues. We believe in the power of youth leadership to create sustainable development.

Our foundation is 80G and 12A certified, IAF ISO 9001:2015 certified, and registered with NITI Aayog, ensuring transparency, quality, and accountability in everything we do.

Projects Creating Real Change

Welfare

Project SEVA

Providing food, clothing, and essential support to underprivileged communities and daily wage workers to ensure no one goes hungry.

Education

Project BACHPANSHALA

Bridging educational gaps by supporting underprivileged children with basic literacy, life skills, and academic resources.

Empowerment

Project UDAAN

Dedicated to women's empowerment, focusing on skill development, financial independence, and essential awareness initiatives.

Environment

Project PRAKRITI

Concentrating on environmental sustainability through massive tree planting drives, cleanliness campaigns, and nature education.
